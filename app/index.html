Why Choose Us?

Because we LOVE animals! As pet owners ourselves, we have extensive experience caring for animals. Nothing makes us happier than exploring the city with all of our furry friends.

What About Safety?

Unlike other companies, we NEVER walk more than 2 dogs. This allows us to give your friend 100% of our attention.

Can I meet you first?

Absolutely! before we take a new furry client, we like to do a short meet and greet. This allows us to get familiar with eachother and go over and questions or concerns

Meet Your Dogs New friend

Hey, I'm John!

I’m experienced with many different breeds and personalities. In the past, I worked in animal care at a local pet hotel and volunteered at Toronto Wildlife Center.

During my time there, I met many of the cities most amazing pets and kept them safe and happy while their owner was away.

My approach is to give animals the respect they deserve and build rapport with them, so they can be as comfortable as possible.
